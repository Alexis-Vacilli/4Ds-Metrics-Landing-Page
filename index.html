About Us

From idea to business

The 4Ds Metrics model is an approach designed for the rapid and efficient development of desirable and feasible product or service to a viable business model. This is achieved by applying a combination of the iterative 4Ds namely; Define, Design, Develop and Deploy as well as bringing into play design thinking. In simple words, 4Ds Metrics represent a process of developing a new business idea to a viable business.

Our Services

Business Diagnostics & Development

Define

Define to understand the customer's needs, so as to find possible solutions; while considering the entire solution space.

Activities covered under Define Stage

  • Business Model Hypotheses, Mapping and Team Building
  • Market research and customer needs analysis
  • Solution Concept Development

Key objectives of the above mentioned activities

  • Making sure the team is well organized and skilled enough to develop the business.
  • Being strategic to choose where, how and who is going to be interviewed during the market research and considering different demographics among the target market.
  • Building a good relationship with some of the customers during the market research in order to engage them throughout the process till deployment.
×

Design

Design a detailed design of the proposed solution and shape the product’s or service’s desirability and feasibility.

Activities covered under Design Stage

  • Competition Analysis, Revenue Model Design & Service Experience Cycle Map
  • Prototype design & Intellectual Property
  • Product Specifications, Tools & Technology

Key objectives of the above mentioned activities

  • Making sure the product is innovative enough to make a difference over the competition and creates compelling values for the customers.
  • Leveraging modern tools and technologies to efficiently design the product or service and engage previously secured customers to test the concept and desirability of the design.
  • Having the product/service Specifications and metrics well analyzed and considering Trade-offs where necessary.
×

Develop

Develop a product or service that serves the identified needs ensuring viability and sustainability of the business, as well as its impact on the environment.

Activities covered under Develop Stage

  • Product or Service Development Process
  • Product/Service Development economics & Financial Modeling
  • Prototype development, Testing & MVP Packaging

Key objectives of the above mentioned activities

  • Working on financial projections and determining whether the product/service will make a viable business or if not, a decision is made to either pivot or change the business idea completely.
  • To never fall in love with your prototype. Instead, putting user experience feedback on the center of decision making regarding the improvement of your prototype to turn it into a desirable product.
  • Engaging already affiliated/secured customers to have their feedback on the functionality and desirability of the product.
×

Deploy

Deploy a minimum viable product (MVP) for customer validation, traction building and go-to market strategy testing.

Activities covered under Deploy Stage

  • MVP Validation Reports
  • Business documents Development
  • Partnerships & Investment Readiness

Key objectives of the above mentioned activities

  • Packaging the product as a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) and deploying it to the early (Especially affiliated) customers for feedback on Price, Functionality, and Packaging before deployment to a bigger market.
  • Documenting everything and focusing on the story, traction and testimonies from the early customers to have a concrete reason for further investment to scale.
  • Looking for strategic partnerships and mentorship to support the scaling and sustainable development of the business.
